About Me

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at The University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Reynold Cheng. I focus on building intelligent systems that bridge natural language and data. My research spans Text-to-SQL, Large Language Model Agents, and Interactive Data Analysis.

I have worked with leading research labs including Google Cloud AI Research, Amazon, Microsoft Research Asia, and Alibaba DAMO Academy, developing benchmarks and frameworks that advance the field of AI-powered data interaction.
